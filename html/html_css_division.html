Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series that revolves around the comedic rivalry between Tom, a mischievous house cat, and Jerry, a clever and quick-witted mouse. Created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, the series first premiered in 1940 and has since become one of the most beloved and enduring franchises in animation. The show follows a simple formula where Tom relentlessly tries to catch Jerry, who always manages to outsmart him using his agility and cleverness. Their chase often involves elaborate traps, slapstick humor, and physical comedy, captivating audiences of all ages. Despite their constant conflicts, Tom and Jerry occasionally demonstrate moments of camaraderie and unlikely teamwork when faced with a common adversary.
